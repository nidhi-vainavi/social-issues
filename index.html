SOCIAL ISSUES

So what is Social Issues?


Social issue is a problem that influences many citizens within a society.
It is a common problem in present-day society and one that many people strive to solve. It is often the consequence of factors extending beyond an individual's control. Social issues are the source of a conflicting opinion on the grounds of what is perceived as morally correct or incorrect personal life or interpersonal social life decisions.
